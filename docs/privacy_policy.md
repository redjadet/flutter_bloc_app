# Privacy Policy (BlocFlutter)

Last updated: 2026-03-27

This Privacy Policy describes how the **BlocFlutter** mobile application ("App") handles information.

## Summary

- The App may communicate with third-party services (for example, map tiles, authentication, and backend APIs) depending on which demo features you use.
- This repository and demo app are intended for development and demonstration purposes.

## Information We Collect

The App itself does not require you to provide personal information to install or open it. Some features may collect or process the following information as part of their normal operation:

- **Account information (optional)**: If you use authentication features, the third-party authentication provider may process identifiers needed to sign you in.
- **Usage data (optional)**: Diagnostic or operational information may be processed by third-party services used by the App.
- **Device information (optional)**: Basic device and app information may be processed for security and service operation (for example, IP address, device type, OS version).

## How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

- Provide and operate the App’s features.
- Maintain security and prevent abuse.
- Troubleshoot issues and improve reliability.

## Third-Party Services

Depending on enabled features, the App may interact with third-party services such as:

- **Google Maps Platform** (map display)
- **Supabase** (backend / authentication)
- **Hugging Face** (AI/chat demo)
- **Google Gemini / Google APIs** (AI demo, if configured)

Each third-party service may collect or process information under its own privacy policy and terms.

## Data Retention

Retention depends on the third-party service you use and your configuration. The App does not include an in-app data export or deletion workflow by default.

## Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent changes.
